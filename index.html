DUPR Estimator

Paste your DUPR dashboard text and get an instant estimate with trend insights and charts.

⚠️ Unofficial tool. This is an independent project and is in no way affiliated with, endorsed by, or related to DUPR (Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating) or its parent company. All data is processed locally in your browser and never sent anywhere.
How it works: The tool reads your past matches and uses only the other three players’ DUPRs and the score. From that it estimates your expected rating: beating higher-rated opponents (or by bigger margins) pushes the estimate up; losing to lower-rated opponents (or by bigger margins) pushes it down. Reliability (how many matches each player has) is not taken into account — this is a simplified model. Everything runs in your browser; no servers, no account needed.
